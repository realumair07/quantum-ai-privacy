Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Quantum AI Platform – Risk & Position Size Calculator browser extension (the “Extension”). Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information the Extension does and does not collect.

1. No Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to see anything you type into the Extension.

2. Information You Enter

Any values you enter (such as account balance, risk percentage, entry price, or stop-loss price) are used only to perform a calculation in your browser at that moment. This information is never saved, never sent anywhere, and is discarded as soon as you close the popup.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

The Extension does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking tools. It does not monitor your browsing activity or the websites you visit.

4. No Permissions Required

The Extension does not request access to your browsing data, your tabs, or any sensitive permissions. It works fully offline.

5. Third-Party Links

The Extension may contain a link to the Quantum AI Platform website. This Privacy Policy does not apply to external websites, and we encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
